Our Policies

To ensure the best experience for all our furry clients and their owners, please review our policies below.

Appointment Policies

Hours of Operation

  • Drop-off: Between 7 am and 9 am.
  • Pickup: Before 6 pm.
  • Late pickup fee: $25
  • There is no overnight boarding of dogs.

Booking & Cancellations

  • Appointments can be made by phone or in person.
  • Grooming appointments are for the day. No specific timeslots are given to a client.
  • Clients will be called once their dog is available for pickup.
  • 24-hour notice is required for cancellations or rescheduling.
  • Less than 24-hour cancellation fee: 50% of scheduled service.
  • Only 1 dog per Grooming Service Order.

Walk-In Services

Select services (nail trims, ear cleaning, anal gland expression) are available on a walk-in basis, subject to capacity. Please call ahead to check availability.

Health & Safety Policies

Vaccination Requirements

All dogs groomed MUST have the following current vaccinations:

  • Parvovirus
  • Adenovirus 2
  • Canine Distemper Virus
  • Rabies
  • Parainfluenza

Proof of vaccination must be provided before your appointment. We work with local veterinary clinics to coordinate and validate vaccination records.

Health & Behavior

  • The shop reserves the right to refuse servicing a dog because of its past behavior or its behavior when the dog is dropped off. This refusal also can apply to the past behavior of the dog's owner towards the shop staff.
  • All dogs are weighed upon arrival at the shop.
  • Please inform us of any health issues, allergies, sensitivities, or behavior concerns before your appointment.
  • Aggressive behavior handling fee: $25
  • Sedated pet handling fee: $25
  • If there is a problem with a grooming, Lucy can approve a credit to the customer's account. If Lucy is not in the shop, then Amanda can approve this in Lucy's absence.

Senior Dogs

Dogs 10 years or older receive special consideration with our Senior Retreat package, which includes adjustments for vision/hearing/medical issues and more frequent rest breaks.

Service Policies

Grooming Assessment

When a dog is brought into the shop for grooming, an assessment of the dog and a review of the dog owner's requests are done before the owner leaves the shop. Every grooming service order is evaluated and an estimated time for all services is determined. The dog owner is given the final price estimate after the grooming assessment.

Special Requests

Dog owners may bring in a picture of what "look" they are desiring for their dogs. We'll do our best to match your vision while keeping your pet's comfort and coat condition in mind.

Photography

All grooming service orders require that a picture is taken of the dog before and after completion of the grooming. These photos may be used on our social media or website.

Pickup Notification

Clients will be called once their dog is available for pickup.

Payment Policies

Accepted Payment Methods

Lucy only accepts electronic payments to minimize the need for her to make frequent trips to her branch of Bank of America. We accept credit/debit cards and digital payment services.

Pricing

Pricing for all services depends on the size of the dog and the condition of its coat and nails. Additional fees may apply for extra services or special handling requirements.
